The Dream of Incheon, the Future of Korea
Since its opening in 1985 as the Incheon Regional Public Officials’ Training Institute, the Incheon Metropolitan City Human Resources Development Center has been actively training professional administrators who play a vital role in the development of the region and offer services to the residents of the city.


The center is operating various forms of education, such as characterization education, long-term education, professional education, and cyber education to reinvent the value of Incheon and strengthen the capability of public officials.


In order to become an educational institute that works alongside not just public officials but the citizens of Incheon as well, we have opened all facilities such as the lecture hall and the gymnasium. Furthemore, we are working to help make the dreams of the citizens a reality through the Incheon Cyber Education Center for Citizen.


We at the Incheon Metropolitan City Human Resources Development Center will do our utmost to foster the best human resources who will realize our view of 「A New Incheon, Happy Citizens」.


I hope for your continued interest and participation.


Thank you.

Kim Bok Ki
