Public officials’ field training between Tianjin,Dalian China and Incheon

Objectives

  • Introduce the history, culture, organization, economic development, and future visions of Incheon and Tianjin, China
  • Conduct class training and field training for basic understanding
  • Discuss multidimensional exchange as a way to improve friendship between both cities

Tianjin Progress

  • July 1996: Reached an agreement for the public officials field training between both cities.
  • Oct. 1996: Conducted the 1st public officials field training (25).
  • Aug. 1997: Conducted public officials field training (agreed to carry out the training every second year).
  • April 2002: Conducted public officials field training (agreed to carry out the training every year).
  • Oct. 2009: Conducted field training seven (10) times for 445 public officials.

Dalian Progress

  • Oct. 2004: Reached an agreement for the public officials field training between both cities.
  • July. 2005: Conducted public officials field training (agreed to carry out the training every second year).
  • Nov. 2009: Conducted field training seven (3) times for 86 public officials.

Major Training Contents

Division Incheon Tianjin,Dalian
Class Training
  • History, culture and businesses of Incheon
  • Economic development and future vision of Incheon
  • Structure of government organization and development; management and cultivation of human resources
  • Urban planning of Incheon
  • Understanding the history of the public officials system of China
  • Policy in attracting foreign companies and investment-related procedures in China
  • Long-term development project of Tianjin, China
  • Understanding the general history and culture of China
Field Training
  • Songdo Promotion Center, Gas Science Center
  • Metropolitan reclaimed land, Cheongrado Crematorium
  • Samsung Electronics, Daewoo Motor, INI Steel
  • Eunyul Mask Dance Exhibition Center, Insa-dong Street
  • Jeju Island Tour (2 nights and 3 days)
  • Introduction to Tianjin,Dalian
  • Economy and Technology Development Zone, Seaport
  • Great Wall, Tiananmen, Forbidden City, Shenyang
  • Jinan in Shandong, Qianfoshan, Bawutucheon

Achievement

  • Set the foundation for friendly cooperation through the mutual and extensive understanding of the history, culture and customs of both countries.
  • Build and strengthen human ties by focusing on an international mindset towards China and improving mutual understanding.
